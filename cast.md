# The Cast — alien forge + 100+ roster

Neila and Pip are the fixed stars (see [characters.md](characters.md)). Everyone here is a **guest**: pick one (or invent one) themed to the concept being explained. Guests are throwaway — assemble from the forge, don't store bespoke art.

## How to use this

1. Read the concept. Pick a roster character whose **best for** matches, or roll your own from the parts.
2. Build its SVG by combining one part from each row of the forge + its signature prop + its hue.
3. Keep the thick-outline, rounded, friendly style. Guests are NOT teal (Neila) or lavender (Pip) so they read as guests.

A recipe looks like: `B3 / E5 / A3 / L2 + beaker, lime` → wobbly body, five eyes, three spikes, four arms, holding a beaker, lime-green.

## Notes / limits

- `ask-neila: parametric forge over 100 bespoke SVGs — same variety, ~5% of the code. Give a character a hand-drawn signature look only when one actually earns it (a repeat favourite).`
- Recipes are guidance, not pixels — assemble guests from the Neila/Pip primitives + these parts. Two eyes on a coral blob holding a book is "Memo"; nobody audits the pixels.
- Need more than 100? The forge multiplies out to thousands (8 bodies × 6 eyes × 6 tops × 5 limbs × 25 props × 14 hues). The roster just names the memorable ones.
